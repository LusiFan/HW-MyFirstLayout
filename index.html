Call me Lusi and be my Fan

UI/UX and Graphic Designer

My name is Lusi Fan, and I'm originally from Beijing, China. I once dreamed to be an contemporary artist until my bank account savings dropped faster than stock market recently, then I started working as a graphic designer and UI designer in Beijing. I came to the States in 2014, after obtaining an expensive and useless Master of Fine Arts Degree, I started working at De Young Museum and Legion of Honor.

I signed-up for UC Berkeley Extension Bootcamp last year while I was preparing to jump back to tech industry. The main reasons of me joing this program is to systemize my knowledge, and get use to industry terminologies in English, as well as upgrade my skills and familiarize myself with way of working in the States. The most exciting part of this program to me is the Front-end Development part, since this is a brand new field for me and coding is the new sexy for being a millennial.
